About the Coach and Gym!


Memphis has a new destination for MMA enthusiasts with the opening of Lior’s, a locally owned gym dedicated to high-level martial arts training. Focused on disciplines like Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Muay Thai, boxing, and wrestling, Lior’s provides an intense and structured environment for fighters of all skill levels. Whether you're an aspiring competitor looking to step into the cage or someone interested in self-defense and fitness, this gym offers expert coaching, top-tier equipment, and a supportive community that pushes you to be your best.


What sets Lior’s apart is its commitment to both technical excellence and personal growth. The gym fosters a strong sense of camaraderie, where members train hard but also support and challenge one another. Classes are designed to build not just physical strength but also mental resilience, with experienced trainers guiding each student through structured drills, sparring sessions, and personalized coaching. With beginner-friendly programs and advanced training options, Lior’s ensures that everyone—from complete novices to seasoned fighters—has the opportunity to develop their skills.


At the helm of Lior’s is Coach Marcus Lior, a seasoned martial artist with over a decade of experience in competitive MMA and coaching. A former regional champion in both Muay Thai and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Coach Lior brings a wealth of knowledge and a no-nonsense approach to training. His philosophy is simple: discipline, technique, and relentless hard work. Having trained with some of the best fighters in the country, he is passionate about developing well-rounded martial artists who can excel both in and out of the cage. Whether he’s fine-tuning a fighter’s striking game or teaching a beginner their first takedown, Coach Lior’s hands-on coaching style ensures that every student receives the guidance and motivation they need to succeed.
